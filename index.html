What is the thock?

Keyboard enthusiasts strive for a

nice low pitched, deep, satisfying

nice keyboard makes when the

keycaps hit the plate, which happens

when you bottom out on a key, aka

pressing the key all the down. Here

at cthulhu we help you acheive this

goal, by sourcing the highest quality

parts the industry has to offer.
